What is ClearMind?

The purpose of this website is to educate those suffering from mental health issues or those who are simply interested in learning about beneficial mental health tips.

Available Resources:

  • Manage Articles - Add, edit, or delete mental health articles
  • Manage Resources - Browse support resources and hotlines
  • Manage Users - View registered users
  • Self Assessments - Track stress and anxiety levels
